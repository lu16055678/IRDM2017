| STUDENTS > Second Year > Tutorials |

2nd year tutorials are held once per month. Your tutor will tell you when and where to meet. A tutor's role is to monitor your progress and be someone to talk to about your work or any problems you are having.

In addition to tutorials, a series of Transferable Skills Workshops are being organised that you must attend.

Tutorials are compulsory. If you cannot attend, contact your tutor as soon as possible to explain why.
