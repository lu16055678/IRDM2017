UCL Women In Computer Science

The Department of Computer Science at University College London is one of the leading research institutions in the world.   We are at the cutting edge of our field and we want to be at the front of the drive to promote and support Women in Computer Science.

Our aim is to to increase the number and influence of Women in Computer Science and Technology industries by:

  •  Promoting the work of our Researchers and Academics
  •  Encouraging Undergraduates and Junior Academics to pursue an Academic career
  •  Support Women & Men at UCLCS in achieving work-life balance
  •  Encouraging GCSE and A-level students to choose Computer Science at UCL
