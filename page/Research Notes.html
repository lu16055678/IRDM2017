To obtain a unique RN Number please email the Research Student Administrator, with the title and names of co-authors. Once the Administrator gives you a RN number then please fill out a UCL template cover page and attach it to your Research Note and send the document over as a pdf. There is also a standard LaTeX template and a two-column LaTeX template.
