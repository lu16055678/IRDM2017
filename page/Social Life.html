Active UCLU

Lifestyle, sport (casual, inter-mural or elite). Check out Active UCLU.

The Student Gym, (Bloomsbury Fitness), is open to All, including "Pay as You Go" or drop-in Membership. Located on 3rd Floor - Bloomsbury (Theatre) Building, 15 Gordon Street, London WC1H 0AY

Computer Science Departmental Competitions

The Computer Science Department runs regular design and entrepreneurial competitions (Via Moodle) in which students are encouraged to test their skills and creativity.  

Computer Science Student Shop

The Department has it's very own online shop where students design the products, decide the prices and help spend the income.

UCL Student Union

All students can belong to the UCL Student Union, which is located in Gordon Street near the Bloomsbury Theatre. The union provides a wide range of facilities and services.

UCLU TechSociety

This is a student society run by students for all Computer Science students, which organises social, sports, careers and cultural events. Please see the Society's webpages at http://www.uclutech.com/ to see what is planned and how to get involved.

Student Central (for University of London students)

You also have access to Student Central, which is a combined facility for all students of the University of London. Student Central is located  on the corner of Malet Street and Torrington Place, near to Waterstone's bookshop, just across the road from the department.

Volunteering at UCL

UCL encourages all of it's students to get invloved in the local community and make the most of the volunteering opportunities that are available.  As well as being a rewarding way to spend your spare time, this experience can also look good on your CV.
