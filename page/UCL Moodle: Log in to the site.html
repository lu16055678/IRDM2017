MyFeedback allows students and their personal tutors to view feedback and grades across modules in one place for:

  • Moodle Assignments;
  • Turnitin Assignments;
  • Quiz attempts;
  • Workshops (for peer feedback);
  • Manual grade items (for offline tasks).

MyFeedback also shows tutors and administrators how students are progressing within modules they teach and support.

Getting help with Moodle

Students

  • Visit the Student E-Learning wiki.
  • Raise issues with your tutor or course administrator first, as they can normally help.

Staff

  • Staff should refer to the Moodle Resource Centre.
  • Raise a ticket in the UCL support system or call the ISD Service Desk on 020 7679 5000 (internal 25000).
