Video Inpainting

MSc CGVI project supervised by Dr. Gabriel Brostow. A user-guided approach to video inpainting - the task of repairing portions of, or removing objects from, a video sequence.

This system uses a state-of-the-art video segmentation technique by Grundmann et al. combined with structure-from-motion data and allows a user to quickly remove unwanted foreground elements with a small amount of interaction in a small number of frames.

Implemented in C++/QT and MATLAB.
