           1st year tutorials are held once every two weeks. Your tutor will tell you when and where to meet. A tutor's role is to monitor your progress and be someone to talk to about your work or any problems you are having.
 Tutorials are compulsory. If you cannot attend, contact your tutor as soon as possible to explain why.
