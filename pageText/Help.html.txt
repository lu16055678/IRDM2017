         Where to go for help

        
 
         
         
        If you are a staff member or current student with a technical query, please log in to the Technical Services Group pages. 
 
        If you are a prospective student with a question regarding admissions, please contact us at undergrad-admissions@cs.ucl.ac.uk 
 
        If you are unable to find a page you are looking for, there are some suggestions here which may help. 
 
        For all other queries, please go to the "Contact" page to find the most relevant person.
