         The Computer Science Staff-Student Consultative Committee (SSCC)
 
         
 
         Agenda for a meeting to be held 1-2.00pm on Tuesday 25th March 2014 in MPEB1.04.

         	Apologies for absence.


	Notification of any other business.


	Approval of minutes of the last meeting on November 20th 2013
- Minutes should be signed by the chair and at least one student representative.


	Matters arising from the minutes of the previous meeting
- Student run helpdesk (Graham Roberts)
- Drinking water in lift lobby areas (JJ)
- Discussion about MScCS/FC option module pre-requisites (Kevin Bryson)
- Space and Facilities in any potential new CS building (Graham Roberts)


	Teaching Matters
- Resubmission on Moodle (Michael Boon)
- Late return of marked coursework (Manoj Vaseekharan/Necati Munir)
- Removal of COMP1001 Computer Architecture as core module (Manoj Vaseekharan/Necati Munir)
- Access to past masters projects for MEng students (Richard Everett)
 - Email communication with MSc FC students (David Lonsdale)
 - Coursework deadline (David Lonsdale)
 - Ranking of MSc FC students (David Lonsdale)
 - Dissatisfaction with compulsory MSc FC modules (David Lonsdale)
- National Student Survey (NSS) (Nicola Alexander)


	Facilities
- Lifting of the food ban in the labs (Manoj Vaseekharan/Necati Munir/Richard Everett) 


	Any Other Business.


	The next meeting is due to be held in November 2014.
