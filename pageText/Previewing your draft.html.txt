         Previewing your draft

        
 
         
         
        
          
           
          Clicking any icon which includes the view icon () will enable you to preview your draft. The preview screen is shown below.
 
           
         

         
          
           
            
             
               Fig. 1. The preview screen
              	


            

           

          

         

        
 
         
       
 
        
        
        
         
        	Select the Visual Preview tab to see your page as it appears on the web. (The List View tab is described below);
	Move the slider to switch between the Live and Draft versions of your page;
	The view menu allows you to see both versions of the page simultaneously.

 
         
       
 
        
        
        
         
        
         Allowing others to review your draft

        
 
         
         
        Anyone with access to the CS Draft workspace can preview your draft. Just tell them there is a a draft available and they can login, select the workspace and preview the draft, just as you do.
