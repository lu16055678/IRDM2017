             We are The Bartlett: UCL’s global faculty of the built environment. We develop new responses to pressing world issues. 
           
 
           
             Somos The Bartlett: la facultad global de UCL del ambiente construido. Desarrollamos nuevas respuestas a problemas mundiales acuciantes. 
           
 
           
             我们是Bartlett: UCL大学具有全球影响力的建筑环境学院，我们为亟待解决的世界问题寻求新方案。 
           
 
           
             Wir sind The Bartlett: UCL’s internationale Fakultät für gebaute Umwelt. Wir entwickeln Antworten auf die dringlichsten Aufgaben unserer Zeit. 
           
 
           
             Nous sommes The Bartlett: la faculté globale de UCL de l'environnement bâti. Nous développons de nouvelles réponses à des enjeux mondiaux pressants. 
           
 
           
             バートレット校: UCLの建築環境学部です。私たちは、世界が面している、緊迫した問題に対する新たな解決策を生み出します。 
           
 
           
             Nós somos The Bartlett, a faculdade global da UCL de Ambiete Construído. Nós desenvolvemos novas respostas sobre questões mundiais urgentes.

             The Bartlett Review
 
            

            The Bartlett is a world-leading, multidisciplinary faculty, united by the radical spirit of UCL. Once a year, we distil that spirit in The Bartlett Review.
 
           
          
          
           
           
             The Bartlett launches new undergraduate and postgraduate courses
 
            

            The Bartlett is pleased to announce a new joint Undergraduate programme and six new Postgraduate courses that are open for applications.
 
           
          
          
           
           
             22 Gordon Street reopens
 
            

            22 Gordon Street, home of The Bartlett Faculty and The Bartlett School of Architecture, has been officially opened by The University of London Chancellor, Her Royal Highness The Princess Royal.
 
           
          
          
           
           
             The Bartlett and UCL Engineering expand to Olympic Park
 
            

            The Bartlett and UCL Engineering will be expanding into premises at Here East on Queen Elizabeth Olympic Park in summer 2017.
