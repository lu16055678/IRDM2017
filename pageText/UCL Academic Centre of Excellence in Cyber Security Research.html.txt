         UCL Academic Centre of Excellence in Cyber Security Research
 
         
          
          

         
 
         UCL has been recognised by the EPSRC and the GCHQ as an Academic Centre of Excellence in Cyber Security Research. 
 
         http://www.ucl.ac.uk/news/news-articles/1204/120402-UCL-awarded-excellence-status-in-cyber-security-research 
 
         http://www.computerworlduk.com/news/public-sector/3348461/gchq-awards-eight-uk-universities-50000-each-for-research-in-cyber-security/ 
 
         The bid for the ACE-CSR included CS academics David Clark, Brian Collins, Nicolas Courtois, Peter Coveney, Yvo Desmedt, Jens Krinke, Jens Groth, Mark Handley, Kyle Jamieson, Brad Karp, Jens Krinke, Jamie MacIntosh, John Reid, Angela Sasse and Shi Zhou. 
 
         Dr Jens Groth would like to thank you all those involved for their help.
