       Year 3 MEng Computer Science

       This database contains the 2016-17 versions of syllabuses. Syllabuses from the 2015-16 session are available here.

       

       Note: Whilst every effort is made to keep the syllabus and assessment records correct, the precise details must be checked with the lecturer(s).

        
         
         
          
         Students on the 3rd year of MEng Computer Science take 4 core CS modules, 2 core modules from their chosen IEP minor and 2 further optional CS modules.
 
          
        
 
         
         
         
          
         
          Year 3 Structure

         
 
          
          
         Term 1 core

         	COMP3004 Computational Complexity
	COMP3005 Operating Systems
	COMP3095 Research Methods
	ENGSxxxP Third year Minor Module 1


         Term 2 core

         	COMP3096 Research Group Project
	ENGSxxxP Third year Minor Module 2


         Term 1 or 2

         	COMPxxxx - Choice of elective module from the available selection of advanced level CS modules below.
	COMPxxxx - Choice of elective module from the available selection of advanced level CS modules below.


         Computer Science Electives:

         	3011 - Functional Programming
	3012 - Interaction Design
	3013 - Database and Information Management Systems
	3035 - Networked Systems
	3058 - Artificial Intelligence and Neural Computing
	3072 - Image Processing
	3080 - Computer Graphics
	6010 - Computer Music


          Note - COMP3058 cannot be taken with the IEP Intelligent Systems minor.
